A Day in the Life

Welcome to my little corner of the web. Here, you'll find snippets of my daily life, thoughts, and musings. Today's piece is a short story about a seemingly ordinary day that turned out to be anything but.

The Unexpected Visitor It was a typical Tuesday afternoon when Sarah heard the doorbell ring. She glanced at the clock on the wall: 3:00 PM.Odd,she thought,I wasn't expecting anyone.

"James?"she whispered, her voice barely audible over the pounding of her heart.

He smiled, that same charming smile that had once captured her heart. Why is he here? After all this time?

"Hello, Sarah,"he said, his voice steady despite the storm raging around them.She stepped aside to let him in, her mind racing with questions.What could he possibly want after all these years?

"I've missed you," he said simply, as if that explained everything.
